Portfolio

Biological Transposon Database

Computational tools were used to analyze large amounts of genomic data and identify and characterize transposons within the four different species genomes. These tools helped convey the types of transposons present, their distribution, abundance, and potential effects on genome organization within each species.

GitHub Repository: https://github.com/sveerisetti10/Transposon_Database

Philharmonica Orchestra Subscription Prediction

The project entailed participating in a predictive modeling Kaggle competition with the goal of forecasting subscription sales for a client orchestra. The challenge was to use historical data to determine the likelihood of past patrons purchasing season subscriptions for the upcoming concert season. The focus was on generating probabilistic predictions, rather than categorical outcomes, to assess the varying levels of purchase intent among the patrons listed in the provided "test.csv" file.

GitHub Repository: https://github.com/sveerisetti10/Duke-AI-Modeling-2023-Kaggle-Competition

Optimizing Prostate Cancer Brachytherapy

Explored a real-world application in prostate cancer treatment at the Memorial Sloan - Kettering Cancer Center, where the challenge was to optimize radioactive seed placement in brachytherapy. It involved calculating radiation exposure, strategizing seed placement to safeguard healthy tissue, and expressing this optimization problem in accessible language and mathematical models, supplemented by Python programming.
